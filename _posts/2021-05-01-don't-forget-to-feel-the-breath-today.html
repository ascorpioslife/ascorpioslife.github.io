Don't forget to feel the breath today,


because,


If I look at someone who is trying hard to breathe,


If I look at someone who is trying hard to find the air to breathe,


I would fold my hands to you to not to take it for granted,


I would beg to you to not to take anything for granted,


So,


please take the deepest breath today,


a breath which will run through every part of your body,


a breath full of gratitude and thankfulness,


a breath which you will always remember,


a breath which these humans are pricing, without knowing that its actually priceless!
